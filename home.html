DEAR TEETOSEWA

When we began this journey on August 6, 2018, I already understood what love meant but loving you gave that understanding a heartbeat. Through the thick and thin, the hard seasons and the joyful ones, the misunderstandings, the growth, you never stopped caring. You never stopped choosing us.

Your motherly kind of love , the way you check on me, worry about me, pray for me, and make sure I’m okay is something I will forever cherish. You love me in a way that feels safe. In a way that feels like home.
We have seen each other at our weakest and still chose to stay.

Loving you is not just a feeling for me.
It is commitment, It is peace, It is growth.
I don’t just want more years with you I want a lifetime.
And that is why this moment means everything to me.
